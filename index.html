About Me

I joke that in my spare time that I'm going to start a mini cattle ranch (you know miniature cows, and wrangle them using a miniature horse and my 8 pound Morkie dog, named Charlemagne) but for now its just nice to have some wide open spaces.

Besides wanting to run a mini cattle ranch, I also enjoy camping, 4 wheeling, hiking, cooking, and reading (and drinking pina coladas, long walks on the beach...oh wait...).
